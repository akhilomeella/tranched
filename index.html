For Investors

The Tranched Protocol

Our protocol manages automatically the priority of payments. With the full chain of security, investors can witness legal, audit and cash flow truths becoming one single immutable value chain.

Senior

The Senior Tranche provides the highest protection against credit losses. Suited to passive investors looking for superior risk-adjusted outcomes: Pension Funds, Insurers, AMs and more.

Mezzanine

The Mezzanine Tranche comes with double-digit yields with some protection against underlying loan defaults. Suitable for specialist funds.

Junior

The Junior Tranche is generally retained by the Fintech originators. Occasionally a share is available to investors comfortable with the risk level.
